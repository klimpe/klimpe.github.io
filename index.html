About

Welcome to Klim Peshkov's personal website, Data Journey. You may look over my portfolio of projects here. I'm now pursuing a career in Data Analysis and Data Science, beginning with a professional certificate in Google Data Analytics.

I'm proficient at the beginning level in R, the intermediate level in Python and Pandas, and the beginning level in Tableau and SQL. I also have a Master degree in natural language processing (NLP), although I am working on brushing up on my knowledge in this field.

On this site you may also find several short articles written during my PhD research, where I used machine learning to detect speech unit boundaries and used a number of metrics to examine linguistic data. Even though I ultimately did not finish my PhD, my enthusiasm for study and making sense of data remains very strong.

In addition to my technical abilities, I have a strong sense of motivation and a commitment to ongoing development. I am constantly seeking for methods to increase my knowledge and advance my job, whether it is through taking individual math sessions or investigating new fields of study. Thank you for visiting Data Journey. Please come back again to learn more about my adventures in the data world.
